A Letter I Wish I'd Said Sooner

💖 Dear Sister,

😔I know I hurt and upset you because of what I said/did.
🙏I'm not trying to make excuses. I was wrong.
💔I understand why you felt hurt, disappointed, or angry, and I'm truly sorry for making you feel that way.
🥺I truly regret my actions and wish I had handled things differently.
🌸You're not just my sister — you're one of the most important people in my life.
📸When I think about all the times you've supported me, cared for me, and stood by my side, I feel even worse about hurting you.
✨I'll work on myself and make sure I don't repeat this mistake again.
🤝I hope you can forgive me, but I completely understand if you need some time.
🌷Thank you for always being there for me, even when I don't deserve it.
❤️I love you, and I don't want this mistake to come between us.
💝From the bottom of my heart, I'm sorry. You mean more to me than any argument, misunderstanding, or mistake. I take full responsibility for what happened, and I'll do my best to make things right. I hope that, when you're ready, you can find it in your heart to forgive me. 🫂💕🌹
🎀P.S. — You didn't have to forgive me, but you did. That says everything about the kind of person you are. I don't take that lightly. 💫
